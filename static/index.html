About

White Light Medical is a start up medical device company founded in Baltimore, Maryland by a team of Biomedical Engineers at the Johns Hopkins University.

Clinical Need

Each year, 500,000 people undergo spinal fusion surgeries as treatment for various spinal deformities. White Light Medical has identified the clinical need for an effective guidance system for the safe and accurate placement of screws within the spine.

AccuSpine

The AccuSpine is White Light Medical's flagship product. Using a proprietary electromechanical system, AccuSpine prevents pedicle screw breaches during spinal fusion surgery and reduces patient and surgeon exposure to intraoperative radiation.

Welcome to White Light Medical

White Light Medical is a startup medical device firm that is developing a low-cost, smarter pedicle probe, AccuSpine, for the accurate placement of pedicle screws in spinal fusion surgery.

Founded in December 2012, our team aims to reduce screw breach rates, operating room time and radiation exposure, and therefore will limit the cost of the procedure while enhancing safety for both patients and doctors.
